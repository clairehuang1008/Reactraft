Design upload onto canvas

Kickstart your project by uploading your web design to the canvas. Watch as your vision appears, ready for transformation into a dynamic web experience.

Add components for design

Elevate your design by intuitively adding web components with resizable rectangles, precisely defining their hierarchy through parent container selection.

DOM tree visualizer

Navigate the architecture of your design with the 'DOM Tree' viewer. Visualize the hierarchy and relationships of components in your design, making it easy to understand and manage the structure of your web project.

Border color / Fill options

Bring your design to life with vibrant precision with the 'Border Color/Fill' option. Select and apply your desired colors to component borders and fill areas, ensuring these styles are reflected in your exported design.

Development suite

Maximize your project's potentional by accessing React code for each component, CSS code for the design page, add and navigate through multiple pages, and invite collaborators with ease. Zoom controls and intuitive design tools empower you to craft and refine your multi-page web application effortlessy.

Export project

Transition from concept to code effortlessy with the exporting feature. Export to obtain React and CSS code, mirroring your design's components and styles. Open in your IDE and jumpstart development, transforming your visual concept into a functional web application.
